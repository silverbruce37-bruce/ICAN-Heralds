## 4. Product Hunt 런치 (영문)

### Tagline
Learn the world through daily Philippine news — AI-powered, bilingual, free.

### Description
```
iCAN Herald is a free, AI-powered daily news app for the Korean community in the Philippines.

Every morning at 8 AM, AI generates fresh bilingual news (Korean + English) covering economy, culture, safety, and Korea-Philippines relations.

What makes it special:
📚 Background Knowledge Layers — Every article has L1-L4 learning layers that break down complex topics from foundation to expert level
🌏 True Bilingual — Switch between Korean and English freely. Vocab chips show both languages side by side
📝 Built-in Quizzes — Quick checks in every layer to test understanding
⛪ Ask Paul-Sam — AI-powered Q&A at the bottom of every learning session
🔄 Auto-updating — Daily news + weekly food/travel features, all automated
📲 PWA — Install as an app on any device

Built for bilingual learners, Korean expats, and anyone curious about the Philippines.

No signup. No ads. Just learning.
```
