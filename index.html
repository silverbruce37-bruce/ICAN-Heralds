By ICAN Herald Editorial · 5 min read5분 읽기

Manila, Philippines – The Philippines and South Korea today officially launched the 'Smart City Connect' initiative, a comprehensive partnership designed to accelerate the development of smart cities across the Philippine archipelago. The signing ceremony, attended by President Marcos Jr. and South Korean Ambassador to the Philippines, H.E. Lee Sang-hwa, marked a significant milestone in bilateral relations, focusing on digital infrastructure, sustainable urban planning, and technological transfer.

The initiative will initially focus on pilot projects in key growth areas in Luzon, including New Clark City and selected urban centers. These projects will leverage Korean expertise in areas such as intelligent transportation systems, renewable energy solutions, smart grids, and digital governance platforms. Officials highlighted the potential for substantial job creation, skill development for Filipino workers, and the attraction of further foreign direct investment into the burgeoning Philippine tech sector.

Beyond economic benefits, 'Smart City Connect' aims to enhance the quality of life for Filipinos by providing more efficient public services, reducing traffic congestion, improving environmental sustainability, and fostering a safer urban environment. Both nations expressed optimism that this collaboration will serve as a model for regional cooperation, paving the way for similar ventures across ASEAN and solidifying the Philippines' position as a key player in the digital economy.

The Comfort of Modern Tradition: A Culinary Homecoming 현대적 전통의 위안: 요리의 귀향

There’s a unique warmth that washes over you when you taste a dish that’s both deeply familiar and surprisingly new. It’s like hearing a favorite lullaby sung with a fresh arrangement, or seeing an old photograph retouched with vibrant colors. This is precisely the feeling Salo-Salo Modern Filipino evokes, transforming classic Filipino comfort food into art without losing its soul.깊이 익숙하면서도 놀랍도록 새로운 요리를 맛볼 때 밀려오는 독특한 따뜻함이 있습니다. 그것은 마치 새로운 편곡으로 불리는 좋아하는 자장가를 듣거나, 생생한 색상으로 보정된 오래된 사진을 보는 것과 같습니다. 이것이 바로 살로살로 모던 필리피노가 고전적인 필리핀 컴포트 푸드를 그 영혼을 잃지 않고 예술로 승화시키는 방식입니다.

In our fast-paced world, where global influences often overshadow local flavors, places like Salo-Salo remind us of the enduring power of our culinary heritage. It's not just about eating; it's about connecting with roots, with stories passed down through generations, and with the joy of sharing a meal. Each bite is a conversation between past and present, a testament to how tradition can evolve beautifully without forgetting where it came from.글로벌 영향이 현지 풍미를 압도하는 빠르게 변화하는 세상에서 살로살로와 같은 장소는 우리 요리 유산의 지속적인 힘을 상기시켜 줍니다. 그것은 단지 먹는 것에 관한 것이 아닙니다. 그것은 뿌리, 세대를 통해 전해 내려오는 이야기, 그리고 식사를 나누는 즐거움과 연결되는 것에 관한 것입니다. 한 입 한 입은 과거와 현재 사이의 대화이며, 전통이 어디에서 왔는지 잊지 않고 아름답게 진화할 수 있음을 증명합니다.

This week, as we highlight Salo-Salo, I encourage you to seek out those dining experiences that offer more than just sustenance. Look for the places that tell a story, that celebrate local ingredients, and that make you feel truly at home, even if it's a home reimagined. Because in the end, the best meals are not just about taste, but about the memories they create and the connections they forge.이번 주에 살로살로를 소개하면서, 저는 여러분이 단순한 영양분 이상의 것을 제공하는 식사 경험을 찾아보기를 권합니다. 이야기를 들려주고, 현지 재료를 기념하며, 비록 재해석된 집이라 할지라도 진정으로 집에 있는 것처럼 느끼게 해주는 장소를 찾아보세요. 결국, 최고의 식사는 맛뿐만 아니라 그들이 만들어내는 추억과 그들이 맺는 연결에 관한 것이기 때문입니다.
