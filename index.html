Photo: Philippine and Korean officials shaking hands after signing a green technology agreement (April 18, 2026)
By ICAN Herald Editorial · 5 min read5분 읽기

The Philippines and South Korea have officially solidified a comprehensive Green Technology Alliance, marking a new chapter in their bilateral relations focused on environmental sustainability and economic development. The agreement, signed by high-ranking officials from both nations, prioritizes joint research and development, technology transfer, and investment in renewable energy projects, smart city solutions, and waste management systems.

This strategic partnership is expected to significantly benefit the Korean community in the Philippines, particularly businesses operating in or looking to enter the green technology sector. With enhanced governmental support and streamlined regulatory processes, Korean firms specializing in solar, wind, and geothermal energy, as well as advanced recycling and sustainable urban planning, will find fertile ground for expansion. Furthermore, the initiative is poised to foster a more eco-conscious living and business environment, aligning with global sustainability goals.

Both governments expressed optimism that the alliance will not only contribute to the Philippines' climate change mitigation efforts but also strengthen economic ties and cultural exchange. Plans include establishing a joint task force to oversee project implementation and regularly review progress, ensuring tangible outcomes. This long-term commitment underscores a shared vision for a greener, more prosperous future for both countries and the wider ASEAN region.

Why Kare-Kare Still Matters: A Meditation on Patience 깐데-까레가 여전히 중요한 이유: 인내에 대한 명상

In a city that moves at the speed of delivery apps and midnight cravings, there's something almost radical about kare-kare. It demands time—six hours for a proper sauce, as Chef Rosa reminds us. It refuses to be rushed, microwaved, or shortcut. In Chef Rosa's kitchen in Malate, you taste what happens when someone decides that their grandmother's way is worth defending, not just remembering.배달 앱과 야식의 속도로 움직이는 도시에서 깐데-까레는 거의 급진적입니다. 시간을 요구합니다—적절한 소스를 위해 6시간, 로사 셰프가 우리에게 상기시킵니다. 급히 처리되기를 거부합니다. 말라떼의 로사 셰프 부엌에서 당신은 누군가가 할머니의 방식을 기억하는 것이 아니라 수호할 가치가 있다고 결정할 때 일어나는 일을 맛봅니다.

Food can be many things: fuel, pleasure, trend, status. But the best food is quiet resistance. Kare-kare, in its humility, says something vital: that depth takes time, that tradition isn't nostalgia—it's an act of love repeated across generations. Each spoonful of Rosa's peanut sauce carries the weight of hands that came before, and the promise of hands that will come after.음식은 많은 것이 될 수 있습니다: 연료, 즐거움, 트렌드, 지위. 하지만 최고의 음식은 조용한 저항입니다. 깐데-까레는 겸손함 속에서 무언가 중요한 것을 말합니다: 깊이는 시간이 걸리고, 전통은 향수가 아닙니다—그것은 세대를 통해 반복되는 사랑의 행위입니다. 로사의 땅콩 소스 한 숟가락 각각은 이전에 온 손의 무게를 담고 있고, 나중에 올 손의 약속을 담고 있습니다.

This is why we eat. Not to fill a void, but to be filled by something larger than ourselves—a moment, a memory, a story that tastes like home. Visit Kare-Kare & Choco not because it's trendy. Visit because you're hungry for something real.이것이 우리가 먹는 이유입니다. 공허함을 채우기 위해서가 아니라, 우리 자신보다 큰 것으로 채워지기 위해—순간, 기억, 집처럼 맛나는 이야기. 깐데-까레 앤 쇼콜로를 방문하세요. 유행이어서가 아니라 뭔가 진짜 음식이 그리워서 방문하세요.
